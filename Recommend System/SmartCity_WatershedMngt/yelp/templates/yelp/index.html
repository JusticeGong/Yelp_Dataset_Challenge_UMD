About the Project

This web application was made as part of the class Big Data, taught at the University of Maryland - Smith School of Business. It is the final project and served as a learning platform for the students to test and understand the different technologies.

Through out the project, we learned the how to condunt big data research. Some of the techonologies and tools used in this app include Django, Python, ArcGis, R.
